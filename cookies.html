Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 17, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

NameMyBrand uses cookies to:

  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide personalized advertising
  • Improve website functionality and user experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Category Purpose Examples
Essential Cookies Necessary for the website to function properly Session cookies, security cookies
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid)
Advertising Cookies Used to deliver relevant advertisements Google AdSense cookies, DoubleClick

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website usage. Google Analytics cookies include:

  • _ga: Distinguishes unique users (expires after 2 years)
  • _gid: Distinguishes unique users (expires after 24 hours)
  • _gat: Throttles request rate (expires after 1 minute)

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies to:

  • Serve ads based on your interests
  • Prevent the same ad from being shown too many times
  • Measure the effectiveness of ads

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies you have and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Google Ads: Google Ads Settings
  • General Advertising: Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, please note that:

  • Some features of our website may not function properly
  • Your user experience may be less personalized
  • We may not be able to remember your preferences
